Features

Let's make it flow.

Documentation

Heres the spiel, ITFlow is the answer to all your IT management prayers. It's like having your own personal assistant, but instead of bringing you coffee, it manages all your client info like contacts, locations, vendors, assets, licenses, domains, and documents like SOPs and policies. Plus, with a centralized system, ITFlow makes IT operations so simple that you'll have time to start that hobby you've been dreaming about (crocheting miniature cat sweaters, anyone?). And did we mention that it improves client satisfaction? That's right - with ITFlow, your clients will be happier than a kid in a candy store.

Ticketing

Do you want to know the secret to becoming an IT support superhero? It's simple - just use ITFlow's ticketing system! You'll be creating tickets, assigning priority levels, and tracking progress like a pro in no time. Your clients will be so impressed, they'll be singing your praises like you're the IT version of Bon Jovi. And the best part? ITFlow's ticketing system streamlines IT support so much that you'll have time to do things like take a coffee break, pet your cat, or even gasp go outside (just don't forget your sunscreen). So what are you waiting for? With ITFlow's ticketing system, you'll be managing IT requests and issues like a boss in no time.

Billing

Listen up accounting crocheters, ITFlow Billing is the best thing to happen to invoicing since the invention of the typewriter (okay, maybe that's a bit of an exaggeration, but you get the point). With features like recurring invoicing, quotes, and automated billing reminders, you'll be churning out invoices like a boss. And let's not forget about client net terms and hourly rates- with ITFlow Billing, you'll be able to manage your cash flow like a pro. And don't even get us started on the comprehensive reporting - it's like having your own personal finance guru who always knows exactly what's going on.

Powerful API

Heres one for the tech nerds - ITFlow's API is like the ultimate wingman for your software systems. It's like having a super smooth operator who can connect to other software like RMMs and CRMs and make data exchange feel like a walk in the park. And let's not forget about the enhanced IT infrastructure management - it's like having a personal assistant who never forgets a deadline or loses an important document (unlike your last assistant who seemed to have a black hole in their bag where all your important stuff disappeared into). So, if you want to take your IT game to the next level.

Password Management

Alright! You password forgetters! - ITFlow's password manager is like a superhero who can store, organize, and share passwords with authorized personnel like it's nobody's business. It's like having a personal butler who never forgets your passwords, never tells your secrets, and always keeps your data safe and secure (unlike your last butler who always seemed to forget which password went with which account). And let's not forget about the AES encryption - it's like having a force field protecting your passwords from all the hackers and cyber criminals out there. So, if you want to keep your passwords safe and your IT systems running like a well-oiled machine.

Client Portal

Listen up, client portal enthusiasts - ITFlow's client portal is like a magic portal that lets your clients access all the crucial information they need (minus the dragons and wizards, unfortunately). It's like having a secret hideout where your clients can view their account information, track ticket status, and access important documents like they're part of the IT squad (which, let's be real, they kind of are). And let's not forget about the enhanced transparency and communication - it's like having a megaphone that lets you shout "hey clients, we're totally awesome and organized" from the rooftops (without actually having to climb any roofs, of course). So, if you want to take your client relationships to the next level and make them feel like part of your IT team.

About

  • 2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Once upon a time, in a land filled with IT woes, a new MSP appeared on the scene. But this wasn't just any MSP - oh no, they were different. They prioritized open-source technology and refused to settle for any proprietary solution like ITGlue that only offered basic documentation. No, they wanted something more - something that could cater to their every business need, from accounting to billing to ticketing. But alas, no solution seemed to fit the bill, so they did what any self-respecting tech nerds would do - they decided to create their own solution from scratch. And thus, ITFlow was born, a solution that met their every requirement and made them feel like IT superheroes (or villains, depending on the day).

  • March 2019

    ITFlow published on GitHub

    After slaving away for what felt like a million years, we finally created ITFlow - the ultimate solution for MSPs. We were so excited about it that we couldn't keep it to ourselves. So, we did what any tech nerds would do and published it on GitHub for the whole world to see. Lo and behold, it was like unleashing a Kraken of awesomeness, and we suddenly found ourselves drowning in a sea of interest from fellow MSPs. We're pretty sure we heard the collective sound of jaws dropping when people saw what we'd created. But hey, we're not complaining - we love being the ITFlow rock stars that everyone wants to be like.

  • May 2021

    We have a team!

    It was just a lonely soul toiling away on ITFlow until Marcus and Andrei showed up like the IT cavalry we didn't know we needed. Suddenly, we had a team, and things started to take off like a rocket fueled by pure tech magic. With Marcus and Andrei's help, we became a force to be reckoned with, leaving a trail of awestruck MSPs in our wake. It's safe to say that we're pretty sure we couldn't have done it without them.

  • April 2023

    300 Stars and counting

    We've been working our butts off on ITFlow, and it seems like the world is finally starting to notice. With over 300 stars and counting on itflow.org, we're starting to feel like ITFlow is the prom queen we always knew it could be. And guess what? We're so close to releasing a stable version that we can practically taste it (it tastes like victory, in case you were wondering). We're pretty sure we're going to have to start wearing sunglasses indoors to handle all the glare from our ITFlow stardom.
